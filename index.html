// ABOUT CFS
One Creator.
Still Building.

ContentForge Studio isn't a course empire or a polished media company. It's one person — working a job, raising a family, figuring out how to build real income with AI — and documenting every step along the way.

I started CFS because every "make money with AI" channel I found was either selling a $997 course, flexing results I couldn't verify, or teaching theory without showing the actual work. I wanted something different. So I built it.

Everything here — the free guides, the prompt packs, the book, the app — came out of tools and systems I actually use. I share what's working, what isn't, and what I'm trying next. No gatekeeping. No fake urgency. Just the real build, in real time.
