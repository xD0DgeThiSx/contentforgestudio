THE CFS CONTENT RULES

The Pattern Behind Every Post

Follow these five rules for every piece of content you publish — Short, post, or long-form.

01
One promise per post

Do not cram 5 ideas into one video. One pain point, one result, one CTA. Clarity beats comprehensiveness every time.

02
Show proof fast

Use screenshots, before/after examples, workflow clips, or step-by-step demos. Tell people what happened, not what could happen.

03
Lead with the pain

Start with the problem your viewer already feels: too many tools, no plan, no traffic, no income path. Pain first, solution second.

04
CTA every time

Point people to the free pack, starter kit, app waitlist, YouTube, or recommended tools. Every post needs one exit ramp.

05
Measure saves and comments

Views are vanity. Saves, comments, clicks, and signups tell you what to build next. Double down on what gets saved.
