Privacy Policy

ContentForge AI  ·  Last updated: June 17, 2026

ContentForge AI ("the App") is operated by Content Forge Studio ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ContentForge AI mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name.
  • Usage data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including which tools you use and content you generate.
  • Device information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostics and support.
  • Payment information: Subscription payments are processed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features
  • Process your subscription and manage your account
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Send you important updates about the App or your account
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Monitor usage to prevent abuse and ensure security

3. AI-Generated Content

ContentForge AI uses the Anthropic Claude API to power its AI generation tools. Content you input as prompts may be processed by Anthropic's systems in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not sell or share your generated content with third parties for advertising purposes.

You retain ownership of the content you create using the App.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Authentication and data storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Anthropic — AI content generation (anthropic.com/privacy)
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Google Play — App distribution and payments (policies.google.com/privacy)

6. Children's Privacy

ContentForge AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.
