THE 6-STEP WORKFLOW

The AI Creator Workflow

One idea. Six steps. Repeatable every time.

1
Idea
Start with one outcome your audience wants: save time, make money, create faster, or avoid a painful mistake. One idea per piece of content — never more.
2
Script
Use ChatGPT or Claude to turn the idea into a short hook, 3 value points, and a clear CTA. Target 45–75 seconds for Shorts, 8–12 minutes for long-form.
3
Voice
Use ElevenLabs or Murf AI to create clean, professional narration — especially for faceless videos. Free tiers on both cover 3–4 full videos per month.
4
Visuals
Use Seedance for AI-generated video clips, or screen recordings + CapCut for fast editing. Stick figure / illustrated thumbnails for Shorts. Reserve AI video for intros and high-impact moments.
5
Publish
Post as YouTube Short, Reel, or TikTok first. Then repurpose the script into a LinkedIn post, Pinterest pin, and email to your list. One piece of content = 5 touchpoints.
6
Monetize
Every post points to one destination: a freebie, your email list, the app waitlist, an affiliate tool, or your book. Traffic without a destination is just noise.
